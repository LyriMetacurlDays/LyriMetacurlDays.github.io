Note: This was the new worst day of the year 2017 (receiving only a 62). It is also coincidentally the 2017 equivalent of 'Trieval', so it might turn out to be the 'Representative Day of the Year 2017' if hell continues and I don't manage to get a proper career-worthy job this year.


The first part of the day was at the Goddess's place. I went home afterwards.

I went and had a meal which the Viscount made for us - beef mince pasta bolognaise, with lots of olives, while 'Godzilla' (2014 movie) was on. We also watched 'Westworld' episode 10 (of Season 1, but there was only one season at the time, so we didn't call it Season 1).

I felt very empty and lost today, due to the end of the relationship and the lack of jobs (and I was not even on benefits at all either). I truly had to start over from square one. Just with a lot more experience, so it is like I had lost the game but had a chance for Round 2 with more experience points.
