Rating:  88  (Best).

Note: Today's theme is a rare type - Fairy Glass - exactly by definition because it is excited (primary Fairy) and bitty (secondary Glass) - and, by the way, it sounds like it is going deeper and deeper into the rabbit hole until it glitches because it couldn't get any deeper.


I got up at 10:30.

I made coffees and updated stuff.

The pay slip came (this time with the increased wage correct, although it was quite a bit less than I had been told; £10.71 instead of £13 per hour). Also, the DVD of ‘The Thing’ (2011) arrived.

I continued to write up more entries from September 2011.

I watched as the original plans for today turned into dust (as in the song by Mazzy Star) and all that was left was a visit to the Viscount.

I had toast (using the last of my bread supply and the out-of-date salmon Philadelphia cheese).

I got a Tariq's curry and went to the Viscount's place. We watched the first episode of ‘A Series of Unfortunate Events’ (new TV series version) and Episode 1 of the Japanese version of ‘Attack on Titan’. We had two cans of Kronenbourg (1664) during this time. We shared some music tracks. His included some ‘Kings of Leon’ tracks and mine included ‘4.5 Billion Years’ (Windpearl), which the Viscount liked too.
