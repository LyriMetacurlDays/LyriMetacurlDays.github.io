August 2014


This month broke the record of diary neglect since the diary gaps of early 2007, or even December 2003 if you consider that most of the days' events were only inferred years later from my email planner at University. This ended up making it very mysterious, but it was probably mostly due to the spare time being taken up by the Computer Game.

August 2014 also managed to match April 2014's record of the lowest minimum rating (the worst day this month got an 88). Also, the ratings were so stable that they served as the worst case scenario for making the charts - when only one of the y-axis ticks is approached. The rule for this is that the touched one and the one above should be displayed. This is actually worse than if it was maximum stability and zero y-axis ticks are touched, because the rule is obviously then to use the two bounding ticks. For one tick, it is unclear which should be the second tick.
