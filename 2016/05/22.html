#GameOfThronesBinge - Season 1


Date: 22nd May 2016

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: Game of Thrones (Intro) Theme (Ramin Djawadi).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s7L2PVdrb_8


Sleep.

Usual morning stuff except for coffee or breakfast. Saving my hunger for the meal later today.

Finished chapter 7 of 'Money Magnet Mindset'.

Shower etc.

Wrote up the diary entries of 20th, 21st and 22nd of March 2009. Began publishing the ones for 11th to 22nd March 2009. DC texted that he is outside before I could finish this.

DC picked me up. We went to Sukhothai in Chapeltown. Filled ourselves up.

Then we went to Bradford and met Tarquin. DC retrieved his bike and I retrieved my guitar and two t-shirts.

Back at 8th Headquarters, I contacted the Viscount.

Then I went to the Viscount's place to watch more 'Game of Thrones'.

We watched 'Game of Thrones' Season 1 Episodes 6 to 10. I will not give any spoilers for those who haven't seen it. I became a fan.

While watching, we had lots of tea (drink) and some beans on toast.

Back home, I also had the usual supper (oatibix, HNSW and yoghurt). I felt like it was a lot to eat today, but that it would be OK...

Diary publishing - I completed writing up and publishing all the rest of March 2009 (which now makes 'Pisaphae' the latest official entry, though I published that one quite a while ago for being the most legendary day of 2009).
