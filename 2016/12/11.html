Sleep.

Work 14:00 to 22:00. On the way, I noticed that the petrol was low but didn't have time to refill.

At the Hive, I had to do a lot of packing correction, and other corrections, which I told a manager about, so I'm not sure if there were any undetected mistakes which my packing partner had done.

On the way back, the petrol indicator hit 1/6 (the last bit because it only displays sixths) and was flashing and beeping on the way back. I had faith that I would have enough to get back. I went to the petrol station on the way back, successfully.

It reminded me of the proverbial rat race - needing to work so you can pay for the car / petrol, so you can go to work (hamster wheel). That's what it was like these days.

I was also listening to this a lot while driving, since it was my favourite driving music at the time:-

Subtheme: Fall to Me (Activa).
