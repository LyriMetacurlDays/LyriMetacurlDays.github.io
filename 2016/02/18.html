Updated some diary stories (3rd to 10th November 2008) on MS Word.

Coffee and shower.

I wished the Queen of Awesome and the Queen of Awesomeness happy birthdays (it's a sheer coincidence that they have the same birthday).

Had a shower (while listening to ‘An Ocean In Between The Waves’ by The War on Drugs, which is rare for these days, but was to commemorate the Queen of Awesome's birthday).

Got dressed and checked everything. Left the house a little late (I had told Pe$ha I would be late).

I got to Door 2.12 (in the Building) by about 09:40. Helped Pe$ha there until about 11:30. I was also asked for help by some other students who happened to be there at the time, so I had no choice but to oblige. However, today I had nothing else to do, so there was no pressure at all. During this time, Pe$ha gave me some food and coffee. I realised that I had not put the newly written up diary entries on the pen drive (they were left at home).

Went to the New Office. I felt since nothing was on today, and helping Pe$ha had counted as my "socialising" for the day, considering yesterday's scare, and it wasn't even 12:00 yet, I now had LOADS AND LOADS of free time. I decided that since tomorrow is fully booked, that day can be called ‘Time Poverty’ and today has to have the opposite word to ‘poverty’. I looked on thesaurus.com and found the closest antonym I could found, which was ‘affluence’. It suited it, I thought. Tomorrow, I changed it to ‘richness’ after a further assessment, but today, on the day, it was known as ‘Time Affluence’.

I decided to write up the memos I had kept in my phone and continue backtracking 2014 entries.

Apparently the only Quake 2 music tracks that I hadn't used were ‘Showdown’ and ‘Descent Into Cerberon’.

I was organising more phone memos and didn't know where to put the one where the Queen of Awesome had shown me the song ‘Ocean’ (Dead Can Dance), so I decided to just mention it here in this paragraph.

I continued organising more phone memes.
