Elemental Exhibition


Date: 29th April 2016

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: White Light (Shura).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_N8mONNW_Ik


I woke up at 07:30 (from the alarm) and had the next Lymecycline tablet at about 07:40.

I had a little extra rest, and had a brilliant idea. I would create a different account with which to collect my income from the postdoc!

I ironed my black jeans and dry-shaved. Then I remembered that yesterday, Amazon had said my new hair buzzer was out for delivery, so it should have been delivered!

I went on Amazon and discovered, to my disappointment, that the mysterious doorbell ring I had heard yesterday evening from the German's flat had been Amazon trying to deliver my shaving equipment! They had completely neglected to RING OTHER DOORBELLS! I thought this was incredibly stupid of them, especially since last year when they should have learned that as a whole.

However, they weren't specifically instructed to ring other doorbells, so they simply rang my flat during a specific time when I wasn't in that particular room, and then decided to give up straight away!!

I changed my delivery instructions, but they didn't have an option to put RING OTHER DOORBELLS! so I entered it into the deliver to a neighbour option - the who? box was other people in the house and the second box, which said something like where, I put Ring other doorbells! (non-capitalised, but still with an exclamation mark). I also changed today's title to be ‘Amazon's Incompetence’, hopefully temporarily, but if they somehow fail to deliver again today, then the title will stick.

Had coffee and breakfast (finished the Jersey milk off). Had shower. Got dressed.

[more stuff in between]

Amazon successfully delivered their package, so the title was no longer ‘Amazon's Incompetence’.

I went to Captain Cheery's house and had dinner there (butternut squash mixture).

Then we both went to Hyde Park Book Club for the ‘Elemental’ art exhibition.
