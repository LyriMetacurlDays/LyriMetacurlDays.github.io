I went to the New Office with the 'A Year of Living Consciously' book, to update the quotes of the days in 2015 online and also to check for driving test dates.

At the New Office, the driving test booking website told me I had logged on too many times and had to do a robot-check. However, I could still look for earlier bookings. One of them appeared right adjacent to (and just before) my current one. However, I didn't bother to switch to that one.

I found this video from 1984 about "how to send an email".
YouTube Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=szdbKz5CyhA

Before I knew it, it was 8pm and I was still in the New Office! Captain Cheery texted asking me where I was.

I went to the After Glow event - while listening to today's theme song of course! I cut right through the centre of Hyde-Park-In-The-Dark on the way (which I hadn't been through for ages, probably since Bonfire Night 2014 which doesn't even count).

At the event, I had some vegan sushi (which tasted really good and sushi-like) and a veggie burger with some raw hummus (all really good!). Santino did a rap.

During the dancing part, DJ Matt put on many songs, including ‘Praise’ (Art of Tones) at some point, and ‘Incoming’ (Motorcitysoul Mix) (David K) at the end.

I helped bring the table and chairs back in.

I went back to 8th Headquarters.
