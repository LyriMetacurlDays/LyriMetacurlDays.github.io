The Lowest Time Pressure Since the Baby Era


Date: 10th March 2016

Rating:  84  (Better).


Music theme: Within the Sadness (TPCi).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Ou_U6DrFJA


Got up at about 10am.

Breakfast: Oatibix and Crunchy Nut. Some peanuts.

Lazy day.

Had a veg smoothie. Again it looked like ‘The Blob’ (1988), but it tasted OK. It had some cherry tomatoes in it this time, and tasted about on a par with a Virgin Mary (drink).

Went to the New Office.

Had butternut squash soup.

Found that the Unilever R&D future leaders placement was unavailable.

Went to meet Tomi at the train station, in the Pret there. We discussed some business things and watched this short movie:-

Validation.
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cbk980jV7Ao

I then went to meet the Viscount. He had a keg of ‘Old Speckled Hen’ beer and some haggis, neeps (swede) and chips ready.

We didn't bother with ‘Attack On Titan OVA’ episodes, although we watched a little bit of the start of episode 1 of those. I showed him the ‘Validation’ video as well as ‘Descent: Underground’ and ‘Overload’ stuff. He agreed that the graphics in the ‘Overload’ trailer weren't so good for these days, and I told him that maybe the finalised version will have much better graphics. This is important because the ‘Descent’ series has historically always been at the very forefront of graphics (‘Descent: Underground’ is expected to be like that), although I guess ‘Overload’ isn't in the same universe.

I and the Viscount watched some episodes of ‘Death Note’ (starting with episode 1). It was made by the same people who made ‘Attack on Titan’.
