2015 - The Year of Living Consciously


This I read a quote from the book 'A Year of Living Consciously' (by G Hendricks) every day, which became the quote of the day.

Despite this, it broke the years-long trend of each year getting better and better (there's no way it could beat 2014). It was supposedly like a viral video called "how my 2015 went", which came out before or during early December 2015, and shows a toddler on a slide where his head bangs on the sides of the slide, harder and harder each time.

I never knew that December 2015 would have an even bigger impact on my head than November 2015, which had been bad enough. Same for the months before, until they had happened. Fortunately, the confusion at the end was also there (rather than in January 2016), that happened since the final impact in December 2015, although apparently those days were not as bad as the worst day of the year which was in February - which, coincidentally, was actually the best month of 2015.
