The Heating Fiddling


Date: 18th October 2020

Rating:  85  (Better).


Music theme: Why Does My Heart Feel So Bad? (Moby).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qT6XCvDUUsU


I prepared for the Leeds trip, including packing the Switch Lite and a suit.

Most of the day, I read more of (and listened to the audio commentary for) ‘The Only Skill That Matters’ and ‘Ultralearning’, but I didn't write it in the diary. I just remember that that was the main thing that happened today, and I kept listening to today's theme on repeat, more than any other day, while I was reading.

Also not recorded, was the fact that Landladi visited, and, along with Wally, the three of us had a massive discussion about the heating and what settings to put it on and when. That is what caused today's title.


Crux question: If I tell a woman You can have a relationship with me if you want. How is that bad? And how is it not good?

Answer: You need an opinion licence for that.
