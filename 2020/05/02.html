Bubblegum Pop Party 1


Note: The title was given a 1 in it originally because I expected there to be more Bubblegum Pop Parties in the future.

Date: 2nd May 2020

Rating:  91  (Best).


Music theme: I'll Fly With You (Blablabla Mix) (Gigi D'Agostino).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NEkMw7wz_eA


I woke up thinking that the Overgod had said I eat too much food which I had stuffed in the fridge. However, it was an illusion (he actually was not here until the afternoon).

I hoovered up the hall, stairs, landing and 4th Headquarters, as part of the flea extermination effort.

Many hours were then spent watching YouTube videos with the Baroness.

The Overgod came in the afternoon and took us to the Concrete Walk, around the nature reserve. I did some modelling there.

On the way back, we bought some Barr bubblegum pop (the drink) and Dandelion & Burdock.

I discovered the song I'll Fly with You (Gigi D'Agostino). There are quite a few versions so I saved the best version and put the Blablabla version today.

The Overgoddess got her recorder and started playing it over every music video that was on, so I didn't get to properly listen to the best version of I'll Fly with You until later.

I listened to some bubblegum pop music while drinking bubblegum pop, so it counted as a bubblegum pop party.

I went to Mt Bump with the Overgod and the Viscount. The Emperor joined us too.

We watched The Mandalorian episodes 7 and 8 at home, then some of the start of Alita: Battle Angel (about the first ten minutes).
