Time Bender


Date: 19th June 2004

Rating:  86  (Best).


Music theme: Sudden Strike Autumn-like Track (Fireglow).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LCUA_hQMlqU


Note: Today's music theme is nicknamed Time Bender because it sounds long but is very short.


Weather: Hail in the morning, Sunny.

At the Space Ship. At some point, I was lying blissfully on the bed in the aft quarters, with the wind softly caressing the curtains. I had the Sudden Strike CD music on track 4 on repeat, with the Yeoman's silver plastic oblate spheroid shaped radio / cassette / CD player, which became the theme of the day.

I'm sure I had it on for a long time, but hardly any time passed on the clock. Like it only played for one time and it felt like it lasted AGES.

That song seems to have a large effect on time, of slowing time down a lot, for the listener.

You can try listening it yourself when you've nothing to do, and see if it feels like ages.

Late afternoon: the Overgod came and picked us up.

Arrived at the Nest and watched the 2nd Half of Germany vs Latvia (result: 0-0).
