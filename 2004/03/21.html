Halo


Date: 21st March 2004

Rating:  94  (Best).


Music theme: Halo (Martin O'Donnell & Michael Salvatori).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uksSdIlJRj8


Weather: Windy and Cloudy and/or Rainy (occasional rain). Spring started.

Started in bed, in 3rd Headquarters, in the Nest. Got up 08:30.

Breakfast: Shreddies.

Downstairs, for a bit.

10:00 - Went on 'DEVIL' and 'Yahoma' for a couple of hours.

Dinner: Marmite Toast.

12:30 - Worked on the Ditch Den at the 2nd Backy Field for a couple of hours.

14:30 - Halo (4 player) at its best. The screen was divided into four sections. Doomguy had the upper left section, I had the upper right section, the Emperor had the lower left section, and the Viscount had the lower right section.

16:00 - Worked on the stick den.

16:30 - The Quest of the Matches. Went to the Nest temporarily, to find matches. With the Halo menu music in my head. The others had gone for a walk, so I broke into and out of the back garden to retrieve the matches.

17:00 - More working on the ditch den. Thomas's little brother came and ruined it all (the fun, not the dens).

Tea: Stir-fried vegetables (pea pods were great, water chestnut, etc.)

Tidied lego up into the attic with the Viscount.

More work in 'DEVIL' - on the space dome in Level 1 of my version of 'Descent 4' (version 3).

Downstairs (TROTK was on Yet Again).

Supper: Crushed Oat Krunchies with Soya Milk. Updated diary.

Prepared for bed. Went to bed.

I have a small sketch of "Overheaven Halo", with different locations in Overheaven. Going around Overheaven Halo, we have: 1st Backy, 2nd Backy, 1st Southy, Behind the Railway, Overheaven Library, Overheaven Station, and Doomguy's House (which is next to 1st Backy again).
