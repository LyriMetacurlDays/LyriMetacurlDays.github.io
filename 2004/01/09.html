A Simple Day


Date: 9th January 2004

Rating:  67  (Good).


Today's weather: Cloudy and Windy.

I prepared for school. Breakfast was crushed Oat Krunchies.

I went to school (on the bus and by walking).

I read some of ‘The Silmarillion’ in room SC4. Registration, a school assembly, and a French lesson happened.

Then there was a Science lesson, then a break.

Then there was an English lesson.

This was followed by a Technology lesson.

Then it was dinnertime. Tuna mayonnaise and cucumber bun, orange juice, salt and vinegar crisps and a blackcurrant jam donut.

I read some ‘New Scientist’ in room SC4 then had registration.

Then there was a P.E. lesson - doing weights.

Then there was a science lesson, with an end-of-unit test.

Then I went back to Overheaven (on bus and by walking) and entered The Nest.

the Viscount and his friend Ryan were in my room. I had some of The Yeoman's sweets and got redressed.

Tea was garlic, pasta and tomato bolognese, with cheese on top.

Supper was crushed Oat Krunchies.

I played some ‘Quake’.

I watched the documentary ‘Living On Mars’.

I got blamed for being mad by the Viscount, and had to go for a walk (went in the library grounds), but got paid afterwards.
