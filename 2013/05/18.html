The King of Queens


Date: 18th May 2013

Rating:  91  (Best).


Music theme: Utopia (Original version) (Goldfrapp).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OB5Qoy8JHdI


I got a coach to Liverpool in the morning, and visited the Princess.

The Princess told the story of the Manky Mitten Girl, who had falsely accused her of stuff.

We watched some episodes of the sitcom ‘The King of Queens’ (set in Queens, New York), Season 3. One of them was about paintball, which I liked because it reminded me of the paintball session I had recently (21st April).

Jesus met up with us also. We went to the Cinema and watched ‘Iron Man 3’, which had recently come out.

We went to a pub (Yates's) and I had a pint of a beer called Icon (the Liver Bird is the icon it referred to).

We went to Hannah's Bar afterwards, for the last hour of the day, and I had a bottle of Corona (which is seemingly the Liverpool equivalent of Leeds's bottles of Sol.


Routine


3 gratitude reasons: The Princess, Jesus, comedy show.
