March 2013


Usually March is one of the worst months, due to being dismal. However, this was an exception, and is still the best March on record today. In fact, March 2013 beat August 2012 as the new best month of my life!

An interesting trend can be seen from the graph - when the days seem to be getting happier steadily, that might be a sign that one of the extreme happiness days will arrive shortly. But maybe you shouldn't trust it to happen because then it might be disrupted or jinxed.
