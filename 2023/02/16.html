The Foreign Holiday


Date: 16th February 2023

Rating:  87  (Best).


The Overgod's 6027th Birthday. He got a birthday card and a Lowenbrau stein from me.

WFH.

The Emperor came early to see me. We watched some episodes of The Mandalorian. Around 20:30, I got dropped off at Overheaven Station (by the Emperor), then got the train to Overhell first. Switched trains. As I went down, a big cloud of train smoke got me and irritated my lungs (probably worse than loads of cigarettes). So I went to the waiting room to recover. Got on the next train, to Manchester Oxford Road, and then another train to Manchester Airport.

After following the directions to the terminal, Manchester Airport was like the Backrooms at around 22:30 (which I texted the Baroness about). Well, there was one person at one of the desks but everything else was empty. I found where everything was. Floor 2 was the one I entered on, which had the departure gates and toilets. The lift and escalators went between floors 2 and 0 only, although I could see that Floor 1 existed since it could be seen from Floor 0 if you look up, and seemed to be some work offices.

I got some food, water and a coffee from Starbucks on Floor 0, then had a walk around. There was a signal on Floor 2 but not on Floor 0. I got a text from Raw, saying which train he had gotten on, and that many were cancelled. I checked if he would be able to make it, and the schedules said that he apparently wouldn't be here until about 4am, due to various cancellations.
