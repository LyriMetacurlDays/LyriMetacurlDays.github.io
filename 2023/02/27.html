I Am The One And Only


Date: 27th February 2023

Rating:  88  (Best).


Music theme: Major Tom (Coming Home) (Peter Schilling).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ggkaj2lRPuM


We stayed at the Prince William Pub until closing time (about 4am), against our better judgment. I and Raw did more karaoke, then went to our hotels again.

Back at the Aquasol, I packed up and had the rest of the salami, cheese and salad toasts.

I only had 45 minutes sleep (05:00 to 05:45) when I got a rude awakening from Raw, calling me repeatedly as if he kept forgetting that he had called.

Got ready, checked out, got the bus to Palma Airport. I heard the theme song of the year 2001 on the bus, which I consider to be very rare these days.

Checked in the little rucksack. Got the plane to Manchester. Found trains back.

I got back to Hgwrtz at 16:45. The Baroness was there also, and I found out that she was due to go to (guess where) Mallorca!

Aldi and Pub (with the Overgod and the Baroness also).

Tea: Risotto. The Emperor visited too. We tried watching some Bad Batch, but it was talked over, so we put something else on (like YouTube). I made a joke to the Emperor at some point for leaning on his chair since it made a snapping sound: You leant on your chair so much that my throne snapped!
