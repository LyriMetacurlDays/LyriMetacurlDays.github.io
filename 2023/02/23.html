Hello Chum


Date: 23rd February 2023

Rating:  92  (Best).


Music theme: Bailando (Spanish version) (Enrique Iglesias).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y4RAKGoLVHQ


Today was pretty cursed.

I took the drying clothes into the living room briefly, since it was cold outside, but noticed later that they had done a puddle, which I was afraid would soak through, so I toweled it up and put the clothes rack back on the balcony.

Sleep.

I heard of the song ‘Aria Math’ (C14).

I booked the Caves of Drach trip for tomorrow, and also told the Jet2 representative about the buzz and run from last night (in which I should have said 'Ello Chum, like in Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl). Also, I mentioned to her, there has been a crazy woman in a room next to mine (not the one from Tuesday) who keeps shouting, doing a single loud bang that sounds like they're dropping a bowling ball every night, and repetitively saying something that sounds like [American accent] A-men. A-men. A-men.

The wifi was off for most of today, due to repairs, but the mobile internet worked and apparently didn't charge (or so I thought). Also, it was cloudy and colder than usual, so no sea dips.

Coffee and ice cream downstairs. There was a guy there who can be called The Chum (who is English, by the way).

There were no visits to Raw today, as he claimed to have food poisoning, or maybe he had eaten too much (all you can eat).

I had lots of pumpkin seeds (since I needed to finish the pack during the stay). I did some Spanish lessons on ‘Fluent Forever’. There was some frustration with the ‘Mega’ app also. The mobile internet stopped, seemingly for no reason, but the wireless was back on by that time, so I didn't lose Internet today.

I went downstairs for a San Miguel. Some football was on at the time. The Chum chattered to me out of the blue and touched my arm. I wasn't sure if he was just being friendly. I finished the beer and went back upstairs, and the day came to an end.
