2000 - The Year of Theme Parks


Not much recorded here, although I remember that I finished Primary School and started High School. In between, I'm convinced that there was a Summer of James Bond movies on BBC2, which was legendary. Although I can't find any evidence that it happened in 2000 or on BBC2 (so maybe it was actually in 1999 and/or on ITV, in which case I really have almost zero memory of the year 2000). Nevertheless, 'The Living Daylights' is the theme for a day in late 2000 anyway because it was decided that it has to be the theme of the year and is set in stone.
