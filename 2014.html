2014 - The Year of Momentum


2014 started off with me going out more and having the most success yet, but then the Crazed spread a dark shadow over the rest of the year. I went through several phases of appearance, including ‘bearded and bald’. Towards the end of July, I got back into the Computer Game and largely neglected the diary for the rest of the year. Though I did go out with DC and visit ‘This is Not Pantomime’ events a few times.

This year also had introduced a large number of new characters, including the main characters DC and the Queen of Awesome. Raw shone brightly by my side.

On average, 2014 was the happiest year of my life (so far). This was due to a combination of factors, such as being at the perfect age, in the middle of the PhD (low pressure for work) as well as being all studied up on positive psychology. There were a few hiccups (as in March and July), but the other months were amazing.
