Trickquidelle

A very small wood in the middle of a cow field, which was supposed to be the site of a legend where a society of cave-dwelling cannibalistic Gollums would capture children and eat them, then the army blew it up. Now there is a sort of crater with some bricks in there.

It was also historically a site where the members of E.V.I.L. would gather and discuss their evil plans.

It was named in a mocking way by the Emperor or the Viscount, who were making fun of the term ‘Ziqidel’ by saying something that sounded like Triquidel, but they didn't define how to spell it, so Lyri invented that it had to be spelled with the most expansive way of writing it to distance it from ‘Ziqidel’ which is very compact.
