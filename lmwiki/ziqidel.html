Ziqidel

A meaningless and invalid word, pronounced Ziquidel. It was accidentally invented in late 2004 when Lyri and the Emperor were playing Scrabble at the Space Ship. The Emperor won, but Lyri's last letters were D, E, I, I, L, Q and Z, which he arranged as ‘ZIQIDEL’ and would have gotten 92 points (plus 50 for using all the letters) if it was a valid word.
