The DogResidue Effect

When you think you have given up thinking about someone but your mind keeps thinking about them anyway for a time. Named after the item in Undertale, but also meaning that you were dogged about your thoughts and now you aren't, there is still a residue of your doggedness.

Usage examples:

Conscious and Subconscious Mind: I have moved on.

DogResidue: But there is nothing else to think about.
