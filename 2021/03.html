March 2021


This month had claimed the planned Music Theme of the Year 2021 already, so the other months had no chance to claim it.

As the lockdowns were dragging on, it felt like the longest month in many people's lives (including mine).

The main events were the SN10 launch and some minor lockdown lifts (hence the beginning of the Neoneolockdownian nanoperiod). An interesting trend that occurred for me was that the Saturdays were all peaks in happiness except for the last one - which ended up being the worst day of the year and the representative day of the year at the same time, making it the only day to achieve both the black and red prizes at the same time, which is evil.
