Quote of the Month: January was a tough year, but we made it.

The Lesser Supermassive Black Hole Problem was very prominent in the first half of the month, and the Right Hip Problem returned for a time too, but the ratings seemed strangely unaffected and stuck at about 84 with very little variation. Perhaps due to maturity or ice.
