April 2021


The new UK record for least rainfall (since 2014 at least), as it was less than September 2014 (22.5 mm vs 20.1 mm). As with many Aprils, this month looked to be banking all its interest on having large fluctuations (in temperature and in emotion). Work-wise, most of it was taken up by the continuation of the Consultancy's Courses.
