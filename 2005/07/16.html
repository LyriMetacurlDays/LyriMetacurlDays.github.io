The Best Day of the Best Month of My Life - Chain


* Today was the new best day on record and also the first day to be confirmed to achieve the status of “the best day of the best month of my life”. Which is subtly different from “the best day of my life”, which was still ‘The Sleeping Policeman’ at the time.

* I am 100% sure that for me there was no better month than July 2005 previously, so this is a definite holder of the prize. The previous holder was not on record, so it remains unknown, but a good bet might be August 1994, which would mean that ‘The Sleeping Policeman’ had both prizes.

* Today held the prize for five years and seven months, before it was finally beaten by ‘Belland Lemon’.
