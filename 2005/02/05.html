Rating:  91  (Best).


Music theme: Fed Net Intro (Starship Troopers 2) (John Morgan & William Stromberg).

Woke up.

Breakfast.

Went to Brodick (via Saltcoats, Ardrossan and the ‘Caledonian Isles’ ferry).

Went back to the Wizard's house the same way as we went to Brodick.

Descent 3 – Level 7. The Wizard was astounded at the level of graphics (note: even though it was a game made in 1999, the Descent series has always been at the very forefront of the gaming industry, and Descent 3 was no exception, including with game A.I.).

Tea – Pizza.

Descent 3 – Level 8 (the snowy one).

Tea Break (previously known as ‘Downstairs Existence’).

Bed. Read ‘Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire’ chapters 8 and 9. Updated (paper) diary. Tried to think of the music from ‘The Aviator’ but today's theme came into my head instead. Sleep.
