2011 - The Year of Maturation


The emotions in 2011 really suited the seasons. For example, it felt very summery in summer, and it fell in fall. Also, despite containing the worst day of the decade, it had the biggest positive jump in yearly average ratings (which had been steadily rising over the previous four years). 2011 was possibly the first year to have an average rating above 80 (and it smashed that target).
