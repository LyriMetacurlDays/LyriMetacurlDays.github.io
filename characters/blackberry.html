Lyri's pet rabbit. When this batch of bunny rabbits were born, around 2001, one rabbit was so curious and explorative that, as a baby, it nearly made its way over many obstacles and right over the fence to the neighbour's garden! Only to be rescued by Lyri. At some point later on, his petship was reclaimed by the Emperor and the Princess, who decided to rename him 'Blackberry'. Despite his curiosity and vulnerability, he was one of the longest-standing residents of the Nest, and lived to a ripe old age.

Introduced: Around 2001.

Species: Rabbit.

Gender: Male.

Hair colour: Black.
