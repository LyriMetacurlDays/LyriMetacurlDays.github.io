2003 - The Year of Camping


I went from Juniors to Seniors in High School. There was a very hot summer this year and we went camping a lot. I started an electronic diary using Microsoft Works and saved it on a floppy disk. I recorded a lot of the year in a lot of detail, but it was corrupted later in the year and I gave up on it, so most of the data is lost. Only a few that were recorded on paper and a few memories remain, which I have tried to reconstruct.


Music theme: Storm the Embassy (Stray Cats).
