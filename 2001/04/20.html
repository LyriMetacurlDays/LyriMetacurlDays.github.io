Discovered Sound Effects


Date: 20th April 2001

Rating:  Unknown .


In the 2001 organiser, there is only one entry I wrote, which is for today, and says simply DISCOVERED SOUND EFFECTS (nothing else). I can only speculate that it's the sound effects from a computer game, most likely Spider-Man Cartoon Maker or Descent. Interestingly, this is the true oldest actual diary entry in my life, although there are documented write-ups of events from before, such as writing up trips in Primary School, which could be said to count. Just that this is the first known one written in something that resembles a diary.
