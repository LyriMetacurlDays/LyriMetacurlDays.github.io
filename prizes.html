Prizes


There are various symbols for days and sometimes months around. Here is a list of them.



Common

Not really a prize but just the usual symbol.

• Normal day with a known date.

※ A group of days, or an day with an unknown date (placed on an approximate date instead).


Monthly

Every month the monthly prizes are given out.

☽ Red moon - Representative Day of the Month (the day with the same theme song as the month).

☺ Green smiley face - Best Day of the Month.

☺ Blue smiley face - Second Best Day of the Month.

☹ Black sad face - Worst Day of the Month.


Yearly

Every year the monthly prizes are given out.

♛ Black queen or solid red queen with a red glow - Representative Day of the Year (the day with the same theme song as the year). This may or may not be different to a Representative Day of a Month, since those can have a smaller but stronger sphere of influence, while these have a wider sphere of influence over the whole year.

♕ White queen or hollow white queen with a red glow - Deputy Representative Day of the Year. Similar to the Representative but slightly less influence over the year or maybe it spread over multiple years. So it may or may not have a wider sphere of influence. Also may or may not be the same as a Representative Day of a Month.

♚ Gold king - Most Legendary Day of the Year. Usually the most memorable or epic.

♔ Silver king - Second Most Legendary Day of the Year. Runner-up.

♔ Bronze king - Third Most Legendary Day of the Year. Consolation prize.

★ Green solid star - Best Day of the Year. This will also always be the Best Day of a Month.

★ Blue solid star - Second Best Day of the Year. This will usually be the Best Day of a Month, otherwise it will be the Second Best Day of a Month (if the Best Day of the Year is in the same month).

☆ Cyan hollow star - Top ten best days of the Year - 3rd to 10th place. Often will share best Monthly prizes too but sometimes don't if the yearly prizes are too concentrated.

🤬 Swearing emoji with a black glow - Worst Day of the Year. This will also always be the Worst Day of a Month.


Rare

Some rare prizes.

♦ Magenta diamond - A day where the theme song (track name) is the same as the title (or an alternate title) of the day. I once assessed these as being about 1 in 500 and intend to keep them very rare.

♦ White diamond with a red glow - Life milestone. There are only 6 possible ones in progressing difficulty or time. Events such as first relationship, marriage, fatherhood (divided into three steps) and the final one is grandfatherhood.


Decadal

Every decade these are confirmed, but they may be updated during the decade too as it progresses.

★ Red solid star - Representative Day of the Decade. This may or may not be the same as another representative day, since its influence is spread over the whole decade instead of concentrated in its own year or month.

⚘ Green plant. - Best Day of the Decade. Also for the month with the highest maximum rating of the decade.

❀ Green hollow flowerhead - Best Month of the Decade. The month with the highest average rating of the decade.

🍀 Green four-leaf clover - Month with the highest minimum rating of the decade. Interestingly, if given to a day then it would be the worst day of the month.

⚘ Black plant. - Worst Day of the Decade. Also for the month with the lowest minimum rating of the decade.

✿ Black solid flowerhead - Worst Month of the Decade. The month with the lowest average rating of the decade.

☘ Black three-leaf clover - Month with the lowest maximum rating of the decade. Interestingly, if given to a day then it would be the best day of the month.
