April 2012


When I discovered the theme of April 2012 (Toby Fox), which is the last one in the April 2012 playlist (below), it was November 2018, and I commented that I remembered that it snowed in April, it had the Titanic centenary, and it had the worst day of 2012. Then, afterwards, I found out that these actually corresponded exactly with the best, second best and worst days of this month. A magnificent coincidence! (Considering that the ratings were already decided on April 2012 and there was no way to change them).
