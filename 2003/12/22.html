Cittàgazze


Date: 22nd December 2003

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: Stealth Frag (Sonic Mayhem).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nwu7QMumCzE


Note: This day might be a few days out. I just lumped them near Christmas 2003.


Approximately around this time, I was into Philip Pullman's ‘His Dark Materials’ Trilogy and was constantly reading it. Read it all in late December 2003. Thought the second book ‘The Subtle Knife’ was the best. Sometimes I changed the music to different tracks depending on which world the characters were in. I forgot which music tracks I used for all the worlds except Cittàgazze (the most high-tech of the worlds), which was today's music theme.

Also the CD from ‘The Christmas Album: A Tribute To Phil Spector’ was on repeatedly most of these days. That was a new fad CD released for Christmas 2003.
