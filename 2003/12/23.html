The New Christmas Theme


Date: 23nd December 2003

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: Parade of Wooden Soldiers (The Showtime Orchestra).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZeN5U-b8mf4


The Christmas CD was on a lot. In my mind, I associated this version of ‘Parade of Wooden Soldiers’ with Cittàgazze. Although, from almost 20 years in the future, I liked to think it suits the world from ‘Northern Lights’, as well as Urik Watterin. I didn't know what the real lyrics (especially the chorus) were, so I made this up:-
