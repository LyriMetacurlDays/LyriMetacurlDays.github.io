December 2003


Nothing was recorded by either video or writing at all. However, there are some things that can be inferred, partly because it was the just before the main diaries began and some impactful events that definitely happened in this month, such as the new 2003 Christmas CD and the reading of the ‘His Dark Materials’ trilogy (by Philip Pullman), which I thought was really good. I also will have played the ‘Quake 2’ expansion pack ‘The Reckoning’ possibly for the first time, and I thought that was really good also.
