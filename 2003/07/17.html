Camelot 4


Date: 17th July 2003

Rating:  93  (Best).


Music theme: Bring Me To Life (Evanescence).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-eGM0IJc70Y


Note: The music theme was decided many years later, because I had to find something that was actually out at the time and very popular at the time, and this one fit the bill better than the others. I'm not sure if I actually heard it at the time, but it seems likely that I would have (and just not paid much attention).


School trip to Camelot.

Went on the Teacups with Bridgey and Nailer (deliberately being tough guys on the least-tough-looking ride). We spun the teacup very fast though, so it was good.

Went on Excalibur 2 (a few times), since that was the obvious top ride.


Note: I remember remembering today's exact date when starting my electronic diary later in Summer 2003 (which was lost because the floppy disk corrupted a few months later and no attempt for recovery was made, and the disk was probably thrown away eventually). So in this instance, I'm sure it was the 17th of July 2003.
