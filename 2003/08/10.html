Stray Cats in the Tent of the Purple Moment


Date: 10th August 2003

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: Storm the Embassy (Stray Cats).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kREKSu77lac


Subtheme: Ubangi Stomp (Stray Cats).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TFyoRKK7AmQ


Note: The date is approximate. It could be off by weeks. However, I have decided to put it on the hottest day of the decade (in the UK), since I remember it was extremely hot on this day.


It was a sunny summer's day. The back garden at the Nest was a nicely mowed lawn.

The purple and green tent had been set up on it.

I rested in the tent, in the nice warm sun, for hours. I was using the Overgod's old red and black 1980s cassette player, listening to some of the Overgod's Elvis tape. It had plenty of other tunes on it though, including some by the band ‘Stray Cats’. This one became the theme of the day because I kept listening to it, and it suited the sunny weather.

At some point, I invented a scary music, to suit a scary insect crawling. The numerical graphic score of it is:-

3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2 3 2 1 2... etc.

Almost like the Gaster theme that Toby Fox made at a later date, but that starts half way through the beat instead and is much less dramatic, but more mysterious.

In the tent, I was reading the ‘Laurel and Hardy’ comprehensive book, which had pictures from all the ‘Laurel and Hardy’ films. Part of today's title was named after the summary of the ‘Laurel and Hardy’ episode titled ‘Their Purple Moment’.

So now you understand the meaning of the title of the day. Which you probably didn't before you read this entry.
