Sub Rock


Date: 16th August 2003

Rating:  90  (Best).


Music theme: Bugs (Pasil Poledouris).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8V1HS0KradY


At Whitby, camping.

Got up.

Played in the tide. We did a dare of going to Sub Rock before the tide came in. We saw that there were loads of sea snails (cockles) and apparently there are places where they are harvested for food. Not these though, we just dislodged some from their sticking places.

Dinner: Tuna sandwiches.

Pudding: Tutti Frutti Fruitini.

Went to look for fossils near the tide, before it came in.

Went to Robin Hood's Bay.

Tea: Fish and Chips.

Looked around Robin Hood's Bay. Played at the boat park. Went down the 199 steps. Bought biscuits.

Went back to Whitby.

Prepared for sleep.

Went to sleep in the tent.
