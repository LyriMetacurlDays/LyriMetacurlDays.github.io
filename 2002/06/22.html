Monster Mansion


Date: 22nd June 2002

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: Return to Scaramanga's Fun House (John Barry).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y4me2IObI1w


At 2am, I had a dream.

There was a mysterious, dark, dusty, messy, monster-infested old mansion (at night). The Emperor, the Princess, the Viscount and I were trying to go in. It was very scary. It had an old, smashed glass door.

In the day time, Professor Lupin (out of ‘Harry Potter’) took me, the Emperor, the Princess and the Viscount inside. The monsters were hiding from us (rather than the other way round).

Every time I went in, a new room was discovered. One time, in the mansion, I had a Walther PPK gun, and Jango Fett and I were hunting each other. I hid behind a black ‘NO HOT ASHES’ bin and poked my gun out, in the dark. Jango Fett came in and took off his helmet to reveal Scaramanga's head. He had two golden guns. I shot him, then I woke up.

I wrote up the entry in my art book.
