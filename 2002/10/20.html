The Jazz Jackrabbit Girls


Date: 20th October 2002

Rating:  Unknown .


Music theme: Jazz Jackrabbit 2 (Alexander Brandon / Robert Allen).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nSiV5TO9qSs


The Overgod took me, the Emperor and the Viscount to a house in Wales. They also had children - a boy (Lorzz, who was the youngest) and two girls (Jai and Spai) who were teenagers at the time (a few years older than me and the Emperor). We played (as children do). Mostly, the Viscount played with the Lorzz outside and I and the Emperor played ‘Jazz Jackrabbit 2’ with Jai and Spai.

We all had a good time. We were instant friends. Instant rapport. However, this was the only day we ever saw them in our lives. I hope I never forget. But I would really love to see them again.


Note: I made this the best AND most legendary day of 2002, since it probably was. Just one day that completely dominated all the rest in the year.
