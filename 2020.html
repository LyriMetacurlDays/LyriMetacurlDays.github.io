2020 - The Year of Hindsight


CET Average temperature:  10.8°C .


They say hindsight is 20/20 vision, and 2020 certainly had so much that could have been done better in hindsight. Except January, that was alright.

Disasters included the coronavirus, lockdowns, job losses, unnecessary financial losses almost rivalling 2016 (despite the actual financial gain being greater than ever also), and to top it all off, the greatest regret of all time (only revealed after it was too late to fix, of course). Despite this, it was also my most productive year in terms of achieving my life goals (so far).
