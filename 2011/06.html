June 2011


June 2011 was one of the most sensational months ever, becoming the new record setter for both the highest average rating and highest minimum rating for any month in my life so far (with the worst day getting an 84). It was a really beautiful and friendly summer, for the most part. In the future, some bullies may have attempted to cast a shadow over it, just with the sole purpose of trying to make me feel bad, but that did not have any effect at the time.

Additionally, I can safely say that June 2011 has the second-best playlist of all the monthly playlists, since it also contains two tracks that were long-standing in my top ten favourite songs.
