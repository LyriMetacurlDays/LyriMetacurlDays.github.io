March 2011


This became the new best month in my life, beating February 2011, and by quite a long shot. This month saw the most significant rise in my base happiness, which makes it perhaps the most important time to pay attention to. It was presumably mostly due to learning some psychology, although if it can be pinpointed to a single day it would be the 4th of March 2011.

This was the first month ever to achieve an average rating in the Best zone (around 86 and above). This month also may have achieved the new lowest minimum rating at the time, but I haven't thoroughly investigated that.
