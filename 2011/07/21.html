Events


The Overgod, the Overgoddess, the Boss and the Wizard were there. I went to the gown hire place to have the Master's costume on.

I graduated successfully.

There was a buffet afterwards, in the Parkinson Building (that was actually the Mechanical Engineering buffet, apparently, although the Chemical Engineering class and staff were all there and we had our photos taken).

There was also a point when I was asked by a TV company (probably LUTV) to give my opinion on accessibility of education, and I think I did quite well, saying that I think everyone should have access to education because there are many hidden genii in the world who would be able to shine if they had education.

After the events were done, the others left. Then I finished reading all of the rest of the book ‘Evolution of Desire’ by David Buss (there was a total of 12 chapters altogether).
