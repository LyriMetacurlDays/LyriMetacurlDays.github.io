Memos


* Superstition about life is abundant and is being revealed and subsequently destroyed, especially by the book ‘The Mating Mind’ by Geoffrey Miller.

* The creative thoughts I had during reading today included the principle of “neediness” being an unfitness indicator – in principle, since everyone is different, an individual would feel neediness towards a particular member of the opposite sex primarily because the “needed” individual displays higher fitness than them. Which means the “needer” is assuming lower fitness.
