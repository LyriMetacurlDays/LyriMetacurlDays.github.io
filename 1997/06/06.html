Sunnyman


Date: 6th June 1997


Music theme: Walking in the Sunshine (Correct version) (Roger Miller).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GbqQ_4Bj8ms


Note: Today's title is based off "sunny" and "the Yeoman".

Today was an idyllic, rose-tinted day at the Space Ship all day with the Captain, the Yeoman, the Emperor, the Princess and the Viscount; playing in the sun, eating outside, and listening to the Yeoman's country songs.

It is, by default, the second most legendary day of 1997, since it is the only one I actually remember apart from yesterday (and the exact date is correct also).
