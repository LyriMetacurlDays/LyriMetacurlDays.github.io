The Spacibo Effect


Date: 15th December 2022

Rating:  94  (Best).


Music theme: Skies Forever Blue (Luxury Elite).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=njgcjGojRKk

Note: The video version (above) seems to have the strange property of being the only video I know of that always auto-activates CCs (subtitles), so if it does that to you, you can switch them off (or not). I thought it was something Toby must have programmed into it, similar to how Tom Scott can somehow program his YouTube video title to display the number of views.


Quote of the Day: Everyone is under a trance. — Me.


There was some ambient background noise all day which sounded like the Deltarune bunker.

I started off having no food or drinks for the first half of the day.

At some point, I had a great feeling, like enhanced senses.

Laughing and listening to lots of music, while nobody else was in Hgwrtz.

There was also a theme of insecurity, but like I was observing myself in an objective light, like a parent (or extremely strong observing ego) as well as emotional sensitivity.

Dinner was crumpets and fried egg.

Later in the day, when the Overgod and the Overgoddess were back, we went to see X Æ A-13, Maiar and the Viscountess. Apparently, X Æ A-13 was shy at first (as I had heard, she gets shy if there are lots of people, but she has never been shy of me personally as far as I know).

We went to Lidl afterwards and got some oat milk and salad.

The Overgod made some stew.

I went to the pub for the quiz (Quizee Rascals team, including Ralsie, Rhea, MEgan, Andras, Sturas and Dave Biscuit). They all had Christmas shirts on, and I promised to come next week with mine on, and they said they'd be there too, but we also heard that the quiz will not be on till mid-January or thereabouts.

I left back to Hgwrtz after the quiz, to see the Emperor. He wasn't there at first, but he turned up. We went for a walk at the hill, with the Overgod. There was a slightly crescent half-moon and not many asteroids (that was yesterday's thing).
