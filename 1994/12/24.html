Christmas Eve 1994


Date: 24th December 1994

Rating:  92  (Best).


Music theme: Fairytale Of New York (The Pogues).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j9jbdgZidu8


Subtheme: We've Got Lots In Common (Irwin Kostal, Debbie Reynolds, Henry Gibson, Dave Madden).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dHTlWuxU3_E


Although I thought this was a lost memory like most of the days around it, there is quite a lot of video footage from this day, so this entry will be expanded on with time.

The Original Trio watched ‘Charlotte's Web’, and this was partly filmed by the Overgod when the song ‘We've Got Lots In Common’ came on, and more clips.

Afternoon - There is a memory I have always attributed to today, although the real date (even the year) is actually unknown, but I might as well put it in today's entry as well: I was walking in Overheaven by myself, near the School, and there was a truck driving around playing Christmas songs, and it played ‘Fairytale Of New York’ by The Pogues. I thought it wouldn't be allowed because it had mild swear words in it and kids could hear it. Although I was already very familiar with the previous track, which says the F word loads, so I didn't care personally.

Evening - The main event was recorded on video.

We opened our presents. The Princess got the biggest present, which was a rocking horse. This became a major thing for the next few years or so.
