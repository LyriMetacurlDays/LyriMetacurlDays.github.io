February 2009


The snowiest month of the 2000s decade. There is a specific February 2009 feeling I get when I think back to this month. One of my favourite months ever. The first half was the snowiest time since the 1990s, as far as I know, which was extra awesome. There was a decent amount of social potential. Pretty much an ideal February.

Interestingly, this month also seems to contain a lot of key days and key themes - including Sandstorm (Darude), Still Alive (feat. Ellen McLain) (Jonathan Coulton), Hotel California (The Eagles), A Shikadi Aire (Bobby Prince) and Militant Force (Frank Klepacki).
