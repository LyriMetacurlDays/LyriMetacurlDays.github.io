Yuletide 2019


Date: 24th December 2019

Rating:  93  (Best).


Music theme: Kill V Maim (Grimes).
YouTube link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xi-V06al_h4


I went to Hgwrtz and watched some YouTube videos with the Duke and the Baroness. The Duke put some cool music videos on, including today's theme.

We had our Christmas Dinner and watched an episode of ‘The Terror’ - probably episode 6.

We all opened our presents.

We went for a walk on Mount Bump South path.

Late that night, I went to sleep in the lower bed of 4th Headquarters since the Viscount had gone.
