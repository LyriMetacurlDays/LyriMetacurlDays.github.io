June 2019


Famous for being the month where Game of Thrones Season 8 and Chernobyl were competing with each other for the greatest TV show (Chernobyl obviously won). Also, Valerie featured as a minor character, despite the almost three year gap.

This month was surprisingly stable weather-wise, in the UK, except for the anomalies on the 10th (when it was like winter) and the 29th, when New Mexico got a giant hailstorm but the UK just got a freak heat wave for the day (out of the lottery of random weather).

It was also very stable rating-wise, with a narrow range between 82 and 89. On top of this, it was the month with the new most high average rating for 2019.

The other thing about this month, as I'm sure you have heard by now if you had been following it, was that the official June 2019 theme playlist (below) was designed to be the most melancholy of all the monthly playlists. The two secret themes are not there because they weren't melancholy enough.
