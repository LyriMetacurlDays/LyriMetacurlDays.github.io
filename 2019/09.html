September 2019


I began attending more regular socials. I also started playing the Quake series again after a decade or more. This month included the time at Orthanc, as well as moving back and forth between different projects within the same team. I lost about 2kg of weight overall, despite the plentiful offers of free food at Orthanc. Temperatures oscillated between warm and cool daily. There was some rain here and there but not too much.

Coincidentally, the rating range was the same as March 2019, and the first day of the month was the best day of the month in September but the worst day of the month in March.
