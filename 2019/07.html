July 2019


Due to global warming, July 2019 was the new hottest month on record (in the UK and probably in the whole world). It included the new hottest day of my life, too.

It was the new best month of 2019, beating June 2019, but there wasn't really much competition, and I expected even better next month.

Also, this month was extremely stable rating-wise - despite technically not being quite as stable on average as June 2019, the only anomalies were a couple of good days.
