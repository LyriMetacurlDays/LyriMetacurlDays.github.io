March 2019


I recovered from the end of last month. Fairground Bridge was installed. Online games with the Emperor returned too, but only for this month.

The ratings slowly crept towards the normal positive spiralled value, although last month's impact was very slow to recover, and I started getting daily headaches this month also (for no known reason, but probably related to an imbalance in the caffeine-water-sleep triangle - a phrase that I just invented right now).

Brexit was the major topic in the news all of this month, since the deadline was supposed to be on the 29th. However, I felt that it wasn't something that really concerned me.
