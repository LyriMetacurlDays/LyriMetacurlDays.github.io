2009 - The Year of Fearlessness


This year, I had my first real taste of driving, and moved from 2nd year to 3rd year in University. I also said goodbye to the Bone, and met the new main characters Raw, Mr Fold and Dravus.

There were many long trips to the Space Ship this year, more so than any other year in my life.

2009 was interesting in that it had a higher frequency of highly legendary days compared to surrounding years. The top two legendary days of 2009 were still both within the top 10 legendary days on record for many years afterwards.
