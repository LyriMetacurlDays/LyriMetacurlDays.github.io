2007 - The Year of Fantasy


2007 was the second-most important year in my natural development (after 2006), and it did have some significant improvement in quality of life, experience and success, but it wasn't really by that much. Ratings were still in a crisis in general (still being lower than 2004 and 2005 on average), except for a few exceptions including the new best day of my life. Most of the general trends (both positive and negative) were still highly influenced by the Overgod. Now since I became 18, there was also a new factor in my life, which was the importance of having children (the number one goal of life) although I thought this could not get started without some kind of basic psychological integrity that needs to be built first. I was relatively confident that something would happen though (at the time), without much effort.

The music theme ‘Baker Street’ (Gerry Rafferty) really obviously suited 2007 more than any other year, and I knew it would.
